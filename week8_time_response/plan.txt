input: total investment (money) f
system: employees
a: 1 / managers (managers = inertance, managers handle the flow of money, more managers leads to faster rate of profit)
b: lawyers (lawyers = damping, lawyers invest in insurance and deal with lawsuits counteracting on profits, more lawyers leads to slower profit)
c: 1 / workers (workers = compliance, workers create value, more labor leads to more total profit)
output: rate of profit (money/day^2) d2x,  profit (money/day) dx, total profit (money) x
a * d2x + b * dx + c * x = f
